Design: Six Functional Layers

A.R.S. is composed of six layers, each providing targeted protection and modular functionality.

Outer Structural Layer

Provides mechanical durability and environmental protection for all internal components.

Hydrogen-Rich Layer

Reduces secondary particle generation that contributes to biological damage.

Absorber Layer

Attenuates deep-penetrating radiation including neutron interactions.

Sensor & Dosimetry Layer

Tracks real-time and cumulative radiation exposure for adaptive response.

Biological Mitigation Layer

Supports cellular repair and reduces downstream oxidative stress.

Inner Comfort Layer

Ensures thermal regulation and wearability for long-term use.

Technology

A.R.S. integrates sensing, shielding, and biological mitigation into a single adaptive system. It is modular, scalable, and designed for both space and medical applications.
