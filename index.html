A.R.S.

Adaptive Radiation Shielding

The Problem

Radiation is a critical threat in deep-space exploration and cancer care. Existing protection is passive, bulky, and biologically unaware.

The Solution

A.R.S. is a modular, intelligent wearable system that integrates physical shielding, real-time sensing, and biologically informed mitigation.
